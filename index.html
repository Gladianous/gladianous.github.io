About Me

My name is Christian Thibodeaux. I'm a recent graduate from Louisiana Tech University with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and a concentration in game development.

Since graduation, I have spent most of my time working, but tend to dedicate my free time to planning and/or creating personal projects in Unity, Godot, or Unreal. I love designing and implementing unique systems, or sometimes just challenging myself to improve an idea I've seen somewhere else.

Floating Islands

A demo project made in Unreal Engine 4 by myself and a classmate. Features 3 small floating islands with interactive elements. Models were made from scratch in Maya.

Custom Renderer

A custom renderer built from scratch in python. One version is fully functioning and another version supports ray tracing.

Fragments of Kyren

A top down action game made in Unity with art assets from a classmate. Made in 4 weeks. Features multiple unique enemy AIs and unique styles of gameplay.

Space Shooter

A small shoot 'em up I made in Unity with art assets provided by a classmate. Made in 2 weeks. This project required me to come up with unique behaviors to add diversity with a very limited selection of assets.

Grid-Based Puzzle Game

A puzzle game built in Unity to be played on Android/iOS with Art and UI assets created by a classmate. Made over the course of two weeks.

Grappling (WiP)

My current project. A tileset-based 2D action platformer made in Godot with a grappling hook as the central mechanic. Still needs some slight polish and art, but I hope to be showing this off soon.

Tileset Light Simulation

A small project made in Godot that attempts to efficiently simulate light propagation in a tileset. Created as an experiment, and I'm rather happy with the outcome.
