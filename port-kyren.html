Fragments of Kyren

Project information

  • Category: Unity

Fragments of Kyren is a project I made with a partner in Unity. It features a hub world that connects to three levels. The first level is focused on combat with small enemies. The second level is focused on atmosphere and avoiding combat. The third level is a boss fight focused on bouncing attacks back. Controls were explicitly given to players, but all further mechanics (reflecting the projectile, which enemy will attack, etc.) were all taught by guiding player attention to great success.
